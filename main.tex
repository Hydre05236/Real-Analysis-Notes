\section*{Preface}
These are the author's notes on real analysis. Our main reference book is Rudin: \textit{Real and Complex Analysis}, the first nine chapters. In fact, I have read some parts of Complex Analysis, but since the content is too analytical, and my current interest is not in such analytical content, I have put it aside for some time and decided to start a separate note on Complex Analysis.\par
It is worth noting that we recommend readers to choose Folland's textbook instead of Rudin when they first learn real analysis. According to my experience of reading Rudin, Folland's content is easier to read, and the content coverage is comparable to that of Rudin (or even broader than that of Rudin, and a lot of the results that Rudin puts in the exercises can be found in the main text of Folland).\par
Due to the author's limited level, mistakes or errors in the notes are inevitable, and I urge readers to criticise and correct them.
